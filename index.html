Prescripchain aims to reduce ‘Doctor Shopping’ by leveraging blockchain technology to create smart medical prescriptions. ‘Colouring Coins’ allows us to create digital assets that contain a patient's prescription. The coin’s journey from Doctor to Patient to Pharmacy gives greater visibility to pharmacists and prevents a patient from abusing the lack of communication channels between doctors.

​

Doctor

Prescripchain enables medical professionals to issue smart patient prescriptions and create a clear and secure method of tracking a patient's medical history.


Doctor Login

Pharmacist

Prescripchain provides pharmacists with a secure and accurate way to check a patient's prescription transaction history and ensure that they are receiving the medication that they need.
