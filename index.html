Prescripchain is a tool to enable doctors and pharmacists to reduce the instances of 'Doctor Shopping' by leveraging blockchain technology to create smart medical prescriptions. 'Colouring Coins’ allows us to create digital assets that contain a patients prescription. The coin’s journey from Doctor to Patient to Pharmacy gives greater visibility to pharmacists and prevents a patient from abusing the lack of communication channels between doctors and pharmacies.
